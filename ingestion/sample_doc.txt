Our company's remote work policy allows employees to work from home up to three days per week, subject to manager approval. Employees must be available during core hours of 10am to 3pm local time for meetings and collaboration.

All expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred. Reports over $500 require director-level approval before reimbursement is processed. Receipts are mandatory for any expense over $25.

New employees receive a laptop, a company badge, and access credentials during their first week. IT onboarding sessions are held every Monday at 9am. Any equipment issues should be reported through the internal helpdesk portal.
